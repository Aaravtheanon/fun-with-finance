    • Service Initiative: Financial Literacy for Support Staff

    Empowering school support staff with essential money-management skills: budgeting, saving, and building financial security.

    Our Financial Empowerment Initiative

    What We Aim to Do

    Our initiative educates the untrained workforce—specifically our school’s support staff—on the importance of financial literacy and its impact on daily life. We are committed to organizing and facilitating a dedicated workshop designed to provide practical financial education, offering valuable insights, interactive discussions, and expert guidance to help them make informed financial decisions and improve their overall financial well-being.

    Outcome

    By focusing on essential money management skills such as budgeting, saving, and understanding financial security, we aspire to empower support staff to achieve greater stability and confidence in their financial future.

    Why We Chose This Initiative

    Our motivation for this initiative stems from a personal commitment to improving financial awareness. We recognize that many individuals, especially support staff, may not have access to essential financial education, leading to challenges in managing money effectively.

    By addressing this gap, we aim to empower them with the knowledge and skills needed to make informed financial decisions and achieve financial stability.

    Our Approach and Process

    Research & Interviews

    To better understand the financial literacy needs of our school’s support staff, we conducted interviews to assess their knowledge and financial challenges.

    Surveys & Feedback

    We developed surveys to gather insights, and used this feedback to refine and tailor our program effectively.

    Workshop Development

    Our primary initiative is a comprehensive workshop, supported by a language-friendly presentation designed to be accessible to both support staff and a wider audience, ensuring effective learning and engagement.

    Inclusive Presentation

    We created materials that simplify complex financial concepts into approachable, interactive modules for enhanced understanding.

    Our Actions & Implementation

    Targeted Campaigns

    We launched targeted campaigns to raise awareness about the importance of financial literacy for school support staff.

    Dedicated Workshop

    Organized a dedicated workshop for our school’s support staff, providing clear and practical guidance on essential money management skills.

    Interactive Discussions

    The workshop featured interactive discussions and educational materials aimed at equipping participants with practical tools for better financial decision-making.

    Key ATL Skills Utilized: Research & Communication

    Our initiative heavily relies on research and communication skills to ensure effective financial education. Through extensive research, we developed an accessible and well-structured presentation tailored for those with limited financial knowledge.

    Strong communication skills allowed us to engage with the support staff, conveying complex financial concepts in a simple manner, and delivering information in a language they understand for meaningful learning.

    Learner Profiles: Principled & Communicator

    Our project was guided by two key learner profiles: being principled and being effective communicators. With a principled mindset, we worked collaboratively, maintaining integrity and teamwork without conflicts.

    Our strong communication skills allowed us to effectively share ideas, engage with the support staff, and ensure the success of our initiative through clear and impactful interactions.

    Challenges & Overcoming Obstacles

    Our biggest challenge was time constraints, as our deadline was fast approaching. Despite the limited time, we remained committed to our goal and successfully executed our project.

    To overcome this challenge, we prioritized effective time management by taking extra classes and dedicating non-working days to ensure the completion of our work, demonstrating perseverance and dedication to our initiative.
