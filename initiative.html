Our Financial Empowerment Initiative

Dedicated to educating and empowering support staff through practical financial education.

Our Initiative

What We Aim to Do

Our initiative educates the untrained workforce—specifically our school’s support staff—on the importance of financial literacy and its impact on daily life. We organize workshops and interactive sessions to provide practical guidance on managing finances.

Outcome

By focusing on budgeting, saving, and understanding financial security, we empower support staff to achieve greater stability and confidence in their financial future.
